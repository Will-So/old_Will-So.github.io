About

Analytical Sense is a technical blog for Data Science and coding. Here I post overviews of projects I am working on, Data Science insights I believe would be useful for others, as well as related topics.

I'm Will Sorenson, a Data Scientist in New York City. I am currently a Data Scientist Fellow at Metis. I'm an economist by training with degrees from the University of Washington (Bachelor) and the University of Munich (MSc) in Economics with concentrations in Econometrics and Statistics. My favorite tools are Python-centric (PyData, Blaze, Dask, PySpark, etc.) and I am also a big fan of Tableau and am well-versed in a variety of other tools.
